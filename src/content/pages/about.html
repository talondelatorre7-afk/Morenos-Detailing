Who We Are

Setting the Standard for Helicopter Maintenance

Creative Rotorcraft Maintenance (CRM) is a premier helicopter repair station based out of Chino Airport (CNO). We provide the highest standard of aviation maintenance services 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We specialize in maintaining and servicing a wide range of helicopter makes and models, including Robinson (R22, R44, R66), Eurocopter/Airbus (EC120, AS350), MD500, and Bell platforms.

Our highly experienced staff is ready to help and just a phone call away. We proudly provide superior workmanship with fast turnaround times suited to meet your rigorous flight requirements. Whether you need a 100-hour inspection, a complete 12-year overhaul, accident repair, or emergency AOG mobile services, CRM has the parts, tools, and talent to get you safely back in the air.

"All employees are responsible for our rigorous safety procedures at all times. Delivering industry-leading standards isn't just our goal—it's our absolute requirement." Jose Rodriguez Co-Owner & Lead A&P
