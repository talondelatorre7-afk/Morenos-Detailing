About Moreno's

Engineered For Perfection.

Moreno's High End Detailing is Riverside's premier automotive restyling and protection studio. We specialize in maintaining and elevating a wide range of vehicles, from daily drivers to track-ready exotics and vintage restorations. We utilize industry-leading techniques and premium products to ensure your investment is protected for the long haul.

Our highly experienced team is dedicated to delivering a flawless, mirror-smooth finish every single time. Whether you need an uncompromising exterior detail, precision paint correction, seamless PPF installation, or advanced dry ice blasting, Moreno's has the tools and talent to ensure your vehicle looks untouchable.

"My goal isn't just to make your car look clean; it's to make the paint look untouchable. We treat every single vehicle that rolls into our shop like it's our own." Erik Moreno Owner & Operator
